What my free time are mostly spent on



Reading Novel

I enjoy reading probably since I was 12. Reading gave more knowledge and place us in different perspective in facing things. I don't usually read Malay books as I found that there's barely any worth to read book with good quality content. Malay authors nowdays often chose to follow the trend of what malaysian readers like. Sometimes it is good to follow the trend but mostly it isn't. Too much trend in reading makes it became cliche and boring as the scene outcome can already be expected without much progress to alleviated into smarter story. It feels like the story became more ridiculous on the verge of stupidity. I enjoy reading light novel nowdays.
